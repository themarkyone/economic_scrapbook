What is this about?

Economics is a different subject from math and science. The mathematic formulas will not easily change, and the scientific rules will always have the same result, but Economics is always changing. These economic changes occur every second, anywhere, in our life. The effects could make everything different. It is very important to follow the changes of the economy, and one of the best sources to keep up with it is the news. In this scrapbook, you are able to find information and opinions on nearly everything we have learned and discussed in the class through the news from February to June 2018.
